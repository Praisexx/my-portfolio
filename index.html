business desk is a platform offering affordable and flexible business registration services, specifically for registering a company with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria. It provides two payment plans: a one-time payment of ₦16,000 or a more affordable installment plan of ₦5,000 for four weeks. The service promises quicker registration times than competitors, with the potential to complete the registration within 7-14 days.
